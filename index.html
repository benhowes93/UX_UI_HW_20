TravelApp

The Problem

Few industries were more impacted by the global pandemic of COVID-19 than was the travel and tourism industry.Travelers have been apprehensive to book trips as they adjust to the new realities brought about by the pandemic.

The Solution

We hope to locate and address the primary anxieties that are preventing prospective travelers from booking trips so that our eventual users can feel confident that they will have a safe, affordable and fun vacation.
My role: UX designer
Tools used:Figma, Miro, Google Office, Zoom

Research

The 20 question survey I conducted with 5 users sought to answer the following questions:

  • What are your primary anxieties about traveling in the post-pandemic era?
  • What kind of traveler is most likely to be influenced by COVID-19 related factors?
  • To what degree are COVID-19 related factors informing their decision on whether or not to travel?
  • What can we do to make prospective travelers confident that they can have a safe, fun, affordable trip in the post-COVID era?

Problem Statement

The global pandemic of COVID-19 has forced travelers to consider factors that they had not previously had to consider. The inability to access and difficulty in processing this information leads prospective travelers to indefinitely put off vacations until they believe that they face an acceptable level of risk.Â 
How might we help travelers process relevant COVID-19 information while helping them organize required travel documentation?

Testing

We tested the low-fidelity version of the app on 5 users.
Our objective was to test the users ability to sign up for the app and purchase a trip. In order to achieve this, we had users complete the following tasks:
1) making a profile
2) adding a credit card
3) purchasing a vacation.

Feature Prioritization

Those results allowed me to discern the highest impact features that the app should initally focus on.

Mid Fi Wireframe

Final Thoughts

We found that users will be willing to resume pre-pandemic travel habits if they feel that the COVID associated risks are low. Addressing their specific COVID associated concerns is essential to restoring their confidence. Our challenge will be to continue to iterate the survey questions so that they can capture the user concerns as accurately as possible, and base travel recommendations off of that profile. However, we have to also ensure that the onboarding process does not become to lengthy, as our competitors all have very quick sign-up/onboarding processes. In short, we have to ensure that users can see the value of taking the extra time in building their profile.
